About me

I am a Technology Enthusiast, currently pursuing MBA from Indian Institute of Management, Kashipur. After a hands-on experience in solving a real-life business problem in Pune, by developing a Service Based online platform called BOOK BAZAAR (thebookbazaar.in), I have learned : Business Development Defining Business Processes Marketing and Negotiation Public Relations I am passionate about technology, innovative products, and initiatives. I am highly focused with a comprehensive understanding of Business Intelligence, Entrepreneurship, Computers, Electronics, and Digital Signal Processing. I have built and published a patent on a device for visually impaired people, to help them walk without a stick in 2018. The device uses a machine learning algorithm to recognize an obstacle and also calculate the distance from the user. It gives an output through the earphones in the form - "There is a chair in front within 1 meter", i.e. it tells the user the name of the obstacle along with the distance.
